This is birdie a unique Assistant...or financial partner.

Our goal with Birdie is to assist with paying your bills, saving and tracking your expenses.

View all of your bills and subscriptions, track what you've paid, setup automatic deposits in amounts ot cover your expense. and receive a notice once its paid.

We also want to make it easy to find old bills you;ve paid, track your spending and provide reports broken down however you like showing weekly, monthly or yearly reports.

Our approach to managing your money is similar to viewing yearly expenses when getting your W-2. You get the overall view of how your money was spent and how much you've gathered year to date.
